F1 Esports

F1 Esports consists of 4 main phases when it comes down to hitting the road for a podium. First, you must go through qualifying, which is open to everyone above the age of 16 who owns the F1 2020 game. If you qualify, you are offered various entry routes into the series. From there, you are in the challengers, a qualification route for avid sim racers. To qualify in challengers, you must complete a series of championship style formatted races. Afterwards, whoever qualifies from the various different qualification routes you will be entered into a pro draft where official F1 teams may pick you to compete in the pro series season.
