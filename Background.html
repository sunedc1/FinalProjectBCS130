The F1 cars used by ferrari racing, from  1950-1990.

Formula 1 racing was introduced to the world in 1950 and still exists to this day. As the years went on F1 cars began to become more and more advanced as restrictions for engines were lifted and more safety, rules and regulations slowly being introduced. Only after the 2000 season would F1 start to see increasing powerful engines as road car manufactureres began to involve themselves in the motorsport. Many of the original teams still race in F1 today and will continue to do so for a long time.
